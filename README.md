## What Pathway Architect Does

Pathway Architect is built for research workflows where simple charts and static result tables are not enough. Inside a single Flask-based application, it brings together miRNA analysis, enrichment analysis, multi-omics interpretation, and LLM-assisted reasoning, helping users move from raw outputs to a coherent biological story.

It is a strong fit for scientists and teams handling genomics, sequencing, and cross-species studies who need both interactive exploration and AI-guided interpretation. Knowledge graph support, natural-language analysis, and plugin-driven extensibility make it easier to examine complex datasets and shape experiment plans.

## Key Capabilities

- Generates AI-assisted biological insight from both structured and unstructured results
- Supports miRNA analysis and multi-omics enrichment workflows
- Connects to a knowledge graph for broader biological context
- Offers experimental design recommendations for planning and follow-up work
- Includes an interactive visualization dashboard for reviewing analysis output
- Uses generative AI and RAG-style workflows for natural-language biological interpretation
- Provides cross-species analysis support for comparative research
- Includes plugin-based extensibility to adjust to evolving research needs
